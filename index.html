What is Qippi?

Qippi is a smart app that makes quoting and procurement quick and easy for both clients and suppliers. Need a quote? Just send a request, chat in realtime, and get neat PDF quotes from the right suppliers (no more email chaos).

Suppliers, we didn't forget you. Respond to quote requests, talk to clients instantly, and get matched with the right jobs in your area. Less chasing, more closing.
